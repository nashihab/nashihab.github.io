Nanosatellite Training Kit

Edu Kit - DIPTO

The DIPTO SAT: Nano-Satellite Training Kit is an educational platform/tool designed to introduce students and enthusiasts to satellite technology and space systems. Led by a dedicated team, this project focuses on developing a compact, functional nano-satellite model equipped with telemetry, digipeater, and weather forecasting capabilities. DIPTO aims to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical applications, enabling hands-on learning in STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics) education. Recognized for its contribution to space technology awareness, the kit provides an accessible and interactive way to understand satellite operations, communication protocols, and orbital mechanics.

AUTONOMOUS DRONE (Ariel Fly)

BRACU RAVEN

Our autonomous drone is designed to provide auto-stabilization in rough conditions and automatic positioning using infrared (IR) cameras and 3D mapping . The drone is equipped with advanced sensors and algorithms that enable it to maintain stable flight even in turbulent or challenging environments. By utilizing infrared sensors, the drone can accurately detect its surroundings and adjust its position in real-time. Additionally, it uses mapping techniques to create detailed environmental maps, allowing it to navigate autonomously and make precise adjustments to its orientation and position without human intervention. This combination of stabilization and positioning technology ensures reliable and efficient flight performance, making it suitable for a variety of demanding applications.

AUTONOMOUS UNDERWATER VEHICLE

BRACU DUBURI V3.0

The BRACU Duburi team strives to create an inclusive community for underwater robotics enthusiasts, fostering an environment where ideas and expertise can flow freely. Our mission is to develop innovative technologies that redefine underwater operations. Duburi is organized into seven specialized sub-teams, each playing a vital role. The Mechanical & 3D Design team handles the creation of physical components, while the AI & Autonomous team focuses on intelligent decision-making systems. Other key teams include Sensor & Circuit, Control & Communication, Operations & Management, Research & Publication, and Outreach (Branding & Design), all working together to ensure seamless functionality. Our vision is to demystify advanced underwater technology, making it accessible to anyone passionate about exploring the underwater world. By collaborating, we aim to push boundaries and make a meaningful impact on underwater exploration and innovation.

Cognysis

Personal Voice Assistance

**Cognysis** is an advanced **AI-powered personal voice assistant** designed to enhance human-computer interaction through **Automated Speech Recognition (ASR), Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Text-to-Speech (TTS) synthesis**. Built with deep learning models such as CNN and TensorFlow, Cognysis can interpret voice commands, process contextual queries, and provide intelligent responses with lifelike vocal output. It features **ontology-based knowledge representation, API orchestration**, and **voice activity detection (VAD)** for precise start/stop recognition. Designed for seamless integration across platforms, Cognysis enables efficient automation, real-time data retrieval, and personalized assistance, making it a powerful tool for both professional and everyday applications.
